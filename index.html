What We Do

BrightFix focuses on simple maintenance coordination for rental property owners. We help keep communication clear, organized, and easy to track.

Maintenance Request Coordination

We help organize maintenance issues from renters so property owners know what needs attention and what steps need to happen next.

Vendor Scheduling Support

We coordinate repair times between owners, renters, and owner-approved vendors so appointments are easier to manage.

Renter Communication

We help communicate updates related to maintenance issues so owners do not have to manage every message themselves.

Follow-Up Tracking

After a repair is scheduled or completed, we help follow up so the issue does not get forgotten.

About BrightFix

BrightFix was created to help rental property owners save time and stay organized when maintenance problems come up. Many owners do not need full property management, but they still need help coordinating repairs, communicating with renters, and following up with vendors.

Our goal is to make rental property maintenance easier, clearer, and less stressful for local property owners.
